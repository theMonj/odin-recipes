Lasagna

A tomato based lasagna, filled to the brim with meat!

This recipe was designed and tailored for your mom, so she can enjoy this scrumptions piece of classic Italian food. It has been Americanized so that when you eat it, you can taste the Freedom bombs dropping on the middle east, ensure our future in this world.

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Meat
  1. Slight Cook your pasta, al dente
  2. Cook your meat in a pan or skillet until brown
  3. Spray a baking sheet with some Pam, or something so it won't stick
  4. Layer your Lasagna starting with Pasta, Meat and then Cheese
  5. Repeat step 4 until baking pan thing is full
  6. Bake in the oven for a minute at 350 degrees for 3 hours
